Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Olives
  • Pineapple
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Mix flour, yeast, water, salt, and olive oil to make the dough.
  2. Knead the dough until it is smooth and elastic.
  3. Let the dough rise for 1-2 hours.
  4. Roll out the dough into a round shape.
  5. Top the dough with tomatoes, cheese, and other ingredients.
  6. Bake the pizza in a preheated oven at a high temperature.
  7. Enjoy your pizza!
